Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Purple Music respects your privacy and is committed to transparency about how we handle your data. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

2. Data Collection

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service. This includes language preferences, liked songs, and premium subscription status. No personal or identifiable data is stored or shared with third parties.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

Purple Music does not use cookies or external tracking tools. We use your browser’s localStorage only to remember your language, theme, and guest session UUID.

4. Payments via Pi SDK

All premium payments are processed directly through the official Pi Network SDK. Purple Music never accesses private wallet keys or handles payments on its own servers.

5. Compliance

Purple Music complies with the YouTube API Services Terms of Service and the Pi Network Developer Policy. We display only public music content via the official YouTube IFrame API.
